Don't settle for that!

Posted on July 7, 2015

Sleep, dream, get up and act; act smartly with all the zeal you possess and don’t settle down for something that does not fit your dreams for settling down is euphemism of failure.

Dreams should never be shackled by feasibility, success is enjoyed the most when is extraordinary. Success I believe is focused energy; it kisses you when you convince that you need it as badly as you need oxygen.

On the journey to near perfection you are acquainted with the scattering factors that try their level best to let you down. They may be successful a number of times; they may pull you back on your intended track. But; do not worry push them and walk; run. Success is inevitable when you know how to get up and slink after a fall.

Success and failure are choices; one has his pick as per the enthusiasm and thirst. Impossible is nothing more than merely a state of mind. You will never relish the sunshine if you are never lost in the dark.

To find out what you want and how you want; introspect. Peeping inside will always be the best option as nobody else is walking your path. Only you know what you can and cannot conquer. The empires are built when you stroke them in your mind. Look inside your soul and see how crazy it is to breathe into that empire.

Dreams do not flourish in comfort zones; at times leaving the bed of roses you need to step down on thorns and perform without bothering the extra effort. It is not the tough time but a tough person who will attain the heights; for he is a diamond that tears away the obstacles without compromising the radiance.

Don’t bury your dream with you in the grave, blow life into it and let it inspire the novices to seek their edges and fly. Make the world an easier place to live by following your passion; kick the conventions. It is the regret that will haunt you but the hardships that will make you feel proud.

Create your life before it creates you!
